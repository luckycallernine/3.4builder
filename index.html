How the AI in this tool works (and what it won't do)

This tool will ask you questions to help you find better facts, and show you worked examples using a different NZ product so you can see how a fact turns into NCEA-style writing. At Achieved level only, it can tidy your own verified fact into one clean sentence — it will never add a number, name, or detail you didn't give it.

It will never write your Merit or Excellence reasoning for you, and it will never invent competitor details or market figures and present them as true. Anything it finds through a search is flagged unverified — that means go and check it yourself before it goes anywhere near your workbook.
